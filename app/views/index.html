This pattern library contains patterns developed uniquely for Home Office services by researchers, designers and developers within Home Office Digital.

These patterns complement the existing GOV.UK design patterns.

You should use them when designing a new Home Office service, unless your research suggests there is a strong case for taking an alternative approach.

Submitting a pattern

To be included in this library every pattern or element must:

  • be used by more than one Home Office service
  • be tested with users and common assitive technologies
  • not be a variation of a gov.uk style
  • be approved by a majority of the HOD design community at a design crit
